  - question: "How can I make a donation to CARD?"
    answer: "You can donate to CARD through our secure online donation portal, by bank transfer, or by sending a cheque. Visit our Donation page for detailed payment methods and account information. All donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act."
  - question: "Is my donation tax-deductible?"
    answer: "Yes, CARD is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. All donations are eligible for 50% tax deduction. We provide official donation receipts for your tax filing purposes."
  - question: "How does CARD utilize the donations received?"
    answer: "We ensure complete transparency in fund utilization. Donations directly support our rural development programs including education, healthcare, livelihood training, and community infrastructure. We publish annual reports detailing our expenses and impact on our website."
  - question: "Can I volunteer with CARD?"
    answer: "Yes! We welcome volunteers with diverse skills. You can contribute through field visits, teaching programs, skill training workshops, or administrative support. Please fill out our volunteer form on the website or contact us directly to discuss opportunities."
  - question: "What are the main programs run by CARD?"
    answer: "CARD focuses on comprehensive rural development through education initiatives, healthcare camps, livelihood and skill development programs, women empowerment, agricultural support, and community infrastructure development in rural areas."
  - question: "How can I partner with CARD as a corporate or institution?"
    answer: "We welcome corporate partnerships through CSR initiatives, sponsorships, and collaborative projects. Please contact our administration team to discuss partnership opportunities that align with your organization's social responsibility goals and our mission."
