## ACT Alliance Membership
Permanent member of ACT Alliance (global coalition of more than 145 churches and related organizations from over 120 countries, committed to delivering humanitarian assistance to impoverished and marginalized communities) facilitating development and relief initiatives across India, particularly in the Northern states, in collaboration with other prominent Churches.

## Trade Marks Registry, Government of India 
Officially registered with Trade Marks Registry (Government of India), ensuring exclusive use of the abbreviation CARD by none other than Christian Agency for Rural Development with headquarters in Thiruvalla, Kerala State.

## NGO Darpan Portal, NITI Aayog, Government of India
Enrolled in the NGO Darpan Portal (NITI Aayog, Government of India) as a voluntary organization registered under the Travancore Cochin Literary Scientific & Charitable Societies Registration Act XII of 1955 in 1977 for the welfare of the poor and marginalized in India.
